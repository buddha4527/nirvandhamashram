About Nirvandham Yogashram

Welcome to Nirvandham Yogashram! Shree Nirvandham Yogashram, nestled in the serene and spiritually rich region of Ranavav, Porbandar, Gujarat, is a sacred sanctuary dedicated to the profound practices of traditional yoga and the pursuit of inner transformation.

The Ashram was initially established by the **late Brahmalin Swami Shree Bhajanprakashanand Giriji in the year 1983**, following a Darshnami Sanyas Parampara. It stands as a beacon for those seeking to deepen their spiritual journey through authentic yogic practices, meditation, and mindful living.

Nirvandham Yogashram is primarily established with an aim to spread the message of our **Vedic Sanskriti**, to teach people about our **Darshanas**, and to guide interested individuals in the field of spirituality, which is considered our true religion. We particularly follow **Vedantic philosophy and Patanjal Yogdarshan**, offering a comprehensive approach to holistic well-being and self-realization.

Spiritual Guidance & Lineage

At Nirvandham Yogashram, we are blessed to follow a revered spiritual lineage, honoring the enlightened masters who have guided countless souls towards self-realization. Our practices are deeply influenced by the profound teachings of our beloved Guruji, **Late Barmlin Sadguru Swami Shree Bhajan Prakashanand Giriji Maharaj**.

We are further guided by the internationally renowned, ex-Sankaracharya and present Mahamandaleshwar **Shree 1008 Swami Satyamitranand Giriji**, who is also the Mahant Head of Bharat Mata Mandir Ashram - Haridwar. Swamiji embodies a rare combination of Karmayog, Bhaktiyog, and Gnanyog, offering profound wisdom and compassionate guidance.

This lineage ensures that every seeker feels supported on their unique spiritual journey, as our spiritual guides impart wisdom through discourses, guided meditations, and personal consultations, illuminating the path of yoga and inner peace.

Yoga & Spiritual Programs

Daily Yoga & Meditation

Engage in structured daily sessions designed to harmonize body, mind, and spirit. Our practices include Hatha Yoga, Pranayama (breathing techniques), and various meditation styles suitable for all levels.

Spiritual Retreats

Immerse yourself in transformative retreats focusing on specific themes, such as silent meditation, Vedanta studies, or intensive yoga workshops. These programs offer a deeper dive into spiritual wisdom.

Discourses & Satsangs

Participate in inspiring discourses and satsangs (spiritual gatherings) where ancient scriptures are expounded, and practical wisdom for daily living is shared by revered teachers. You can find materials like satsangs, pravacans, and photos connected to our Guruji.

Wellness & Healing Sessions

Discover holistic well-being through special sessions aimed at physical and mental rejuvenation, complementing your yogic practice.

For detailed schedules and upcoming retreat dates, please contact us.

Ashram Life Experience

Life at Nirvandham Yogashram is an opportunity to embrace simplicity, mindfulness, and community living. We offer humble and comfortable accommodation designed to facilitate introspection and peace, allowing guests to disconnect from worldly distractions.

All meals provided are sattvic – wholesome, vegetarian food prepared with love and care, promoting purity of body and mind. The daily routine includes collective prayers, yoga, meditation, selfless service (karma yoga), and periods of reflection, fostering a disciplined yet enriching environment.

Our aim is to provide a supportive atmosphere where individuals can experience a deeper connection with themselves and the spiritual essence of life.
